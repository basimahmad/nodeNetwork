About

Find Out More About Us

Node Network is the most secure way to store your funds, earn interest, and contribute to a cryptocurrency network while holding a digital asset for potential long-term capital gains. We do this by having a sustainable and profitable node server rental program on the blockchain ecosystem validating transactions and governing the whole ecosystem also known as masternode. Due to the increased responsibilities handled by a masternode, they are usually entitled to a portion of fees from each transaction relayed over the network and in some cases, a portion of the block reward. This is where the earning interest part of masternodes comes into play.

Think of a masternode as a savings account with a minimum deposit of 1,000 DASH. A traditional savings account pays interest, and a masternode pays rewards, which are very much like interest. In the case of a masternode, the reward (or interest) comes from performing services for the network. Not from lending. The big difference between a traditional savings account and a masternode is that your initial deposit never leaves your possession.

Shared Node

A shared node is a program created to help small investors participate in a server rental program. Their capital forms a pool which is used to build and host a full node which then becomes a larger part of the block chain network, performing specific tasks and in return all participants get compensated accordingly.
